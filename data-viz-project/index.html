Diversity in the Premier Soccer League

By Ashley Burkett

November 21, 2025

The Premier League published workforce data which covers aspects of diversity, including age, gender, sexual orientation, gender identity, disability and ethnicity. The data was aimed at identifying underrepresented groups within the industry for future steps towards inclusion, according to the Premier League. Despite progress made since the “No Room for Racism” initiative, which was enacted in April, the League remains a white, male-dominated workforce.
